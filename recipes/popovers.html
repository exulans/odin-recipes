Popovers

Description

Soft airy popovers, serve with bramble jam or honey butter. Popover pan with 6 cavities is required.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • ¾ cup milk
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 tablespoon melted unsalted butter (plus more softened butter for pan)
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon rosemary (or other suitable herb)
  • ½ teaspoon table salt

Steps

  1. Place oven rack in lower third of oven and preheat to 375°F.
  2. Butter cavities in popover pan.
  3. Whisk eggs, milk, and water in a large bowl.
  4. Add melted butter in a stream while continuing to whisk.
  5. Add flour, rosemary, and salt and whisk until batter is smooth.
  6. Divide batter evenly between cavities.
  8. Bake rack in lower third of oven until golden brown: 45-50 minutes. Do not open until done.
